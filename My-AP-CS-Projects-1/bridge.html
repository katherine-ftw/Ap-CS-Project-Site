Bridge Design #1

For our bridge design project, we were assigned to create a bridge that cars could drive across, could withstand pressure, and would be efficient. Some design constraints that we experienced was the maximum dimension of 400 cm and the material restrictions. For our first draft of the bridge, we hand cut the pieces instead of laser cutting. The mass of it was 128g, and it wasn't able to hold any weight (lol). Nothing really worked well, but a lot went wrong. The bridge itself was very flimsy and our connections weren't sturdy. So, for ver. 2, we are planning to laser cut pieces that can fit nicely together that will solve most of our problems (hopefully). Video here:
